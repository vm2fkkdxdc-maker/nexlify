Who We Are

The gap between
vision and capital, closed.

Nexlify is a regional development strategy practice based in Layton, Utah. We work at the intersection of three groups that rarely speak the same language: city and county governments, private developers and brokers, and the public financing apparatus — state agencies, federal programs, and CDFIs.

We are not a broker. We don't earn commissions, and we don't represent buyers or sellers. That independence is precisely why the government side trusts us — and why the private side can pay us without conflicts of interest.

Our edge is combining AI-driven analysis with genuine, hands-on fluency in public financing. There are many AI real estate tools doing comp analysis and lead scoring. There are effectively none doing AI-powered public-private deal stacking with a practitioner who has actually administered SSBCI and HOME funds. That's us.

  • ✓Independent judgment — no commissions, no conflicts
  • ✓AI workflows that compress weeks of analysis into days
  • ✓Real public financing expertise: SSBCI, TIF, LIHTC, HOME, CRA, Opportunity Zones

What We Do

Three tiers of engagement.
Each one built to feed the next.

01

Regional Opportunity Assessment

We take a city, county, or sub-area and produce a single authoritative document: what this place has, what it needs, the three to five highest-leverage development opportunities, what each would cost and return, and the financing stack that makes each one pencil.

Cities and counties buy this to sharpen their economic development agenda. Developers buy it when scouting a new market.

  • Parcel, GIS, demographic & economic data synthesis
  • Zoning, land use & infrastructure gap analysis
  • Comparable transaction review
  • Public financing scenario modeling
  • Community-facing plain-language summary
Start an assessment →
02

Deal Feasibility & Stacking Analysis

A developer or broker brings us a specific site or project. We produce the financial model, entitlement risk assessment, public financing options, and a recommended path forward — complete with the documentation that typically consumes junior analyst time at a consulting firm.

  • Pro forma financial modeling & scenario analysis
  • Entitlement risk & timeline assessment
  • Full public financing stack: SSBCI, TIF, CRA, LIHTC, Opportunity Zones, HOME, infrastructure districts
  • Recommended deal structure & path memo
Bring us a deal →
03

Ongoing Strategic Advisory

A monthly retainer engagement for cities, developers, or a brokerage's commercial division. We provide continuous pipeline review, deal sourcing, and strategy — the kind of institutional knowledge and responsiveness that only comes from someone deeply embedded in your market.

This tier is where the real ongoing relationship lives. It's not sold cold — it comes from clients who already engaged at Tier 1 or Tier 2 and want to keep going.

  • Monthly pipeline & portfolio review
  • Deal sourcing and market intelligence
  • On-call strategy and financing guidance
  • Stakeholder presentation support
